# Zex'lyn 'Raven' Hakim

*Meet Zex'lyn "Raven" Hakim, the 320-year-old Mothari keeping the lights on at the edge of the galaxy — and looking absolutely stunning while doing it. By day she's guiding lost ships to safety from her post at Ancestor Worship Station and hauling vital water supplies across the colonies in her trusty (if a little rough around the edges) Rusty Runner, but when the cargo hold is empty, this fuzzy-winged sweetheart is either schooling rookie spacers on the art of survival or adding another wild alien artifact to her ever-growing collection. And yes, those wings aren't just for flying — catch her zero-G burlesque set and you'll understand why deep space has never looked so good.*
